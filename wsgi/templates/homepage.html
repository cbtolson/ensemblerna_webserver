EnsembleRNA

A webpage for the visualization of RNA structural ensembles





Why use EnsembleRNA?

A majority of RNAs adopt multiple conformations. All of the conformations that a single RNA may sample in a cell are the structural ensemble. RNA structure is most accurately visualized in this ensemble space. Visualization of the relationships between structures in an ensemble is key to understanding the effects of mutation or environment on RNA folding, stability and function. This ensemble approach to RNA structure is particularly useful in understanding the results from structure probing experiments that collect information at an ensemble level.
